Games

In game’s track, we’ll be writing interactive, graphical games: Pong and Mario. We’ll be using the Lua language and the LÖVE framework.

Pong

Ping-Pong is a traditional player VS AI game. There will be two paddles and a ball bouncing between them. It starts to serve by each player alternatively. If the ball passes the paddle, you can score a point, the game ends if one of the players can score ten points.

Mario

In Mario, we are building a RPG game that simulates the classic Super Mario Brothers game. We’ll create a game world with tiles and blocks and a character that we can move around. Our winning condition is that moving Mario to the right-side of the map, jump to the pole and pull down the flag. If Mario falls into the hole or killed by his enemy, he dies and game over.
