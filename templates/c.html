C

This course starts with programming language C. It starts from Hello world program , then describes the data types and operators of C. It then moves on to array ,struct and strings. After that, I come to Searching like linear search, binary search, and sorting method like bubble sort, selection sort ,Merge sort and study their efficiency using big O notation. After studying the basics, it moves to Pointers and Linked Lists.
